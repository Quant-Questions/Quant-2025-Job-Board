### Quant Resources 
- **[QuantQuestionsIO](https://quantquestions.io)**:
  - Targeted studying for the quant interview with questions with topics including brainteasers, probability and more - most questions here are free. 
- **[Jane Street Question Bank](https://quantquestions.io/playlist/jane-street)**:
  - Have an interview at Jane street coming up, or just want to see what they ask? access for the Jane Street Interview playlist. 
- **[Probability & Markets Guide by Jane Street](https://www.janestreet.com/static/pdfs/trading-interview.pdf)**:
  - General topics to know for interviews at quant trading firms like Jane Street. Great baseline introduction to the topics tested in quant interviews.
